Distance Formula Between Two Points in n-Dimensional Space

$$d = \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^{n} (b_i - a_i)^2}$$

Where:

  • $a_i$ is the $i$-th coordinate of point A
  • $b_i$ is the $i$-th coordinate of point B
  • $\sum_{i=1}^{n}$ represents the sum of terms from $i=1$ to $n$.
